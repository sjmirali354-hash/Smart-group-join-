Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Smart Group Join is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

By using Smart Group Join, you also agree to our Terms of Service, which contain important information about group submissions, referrals, coins, featured promotions, advertising, moderation, user responsibilities, and platform rules.

Data We Collect

  • A randomly generated device key to identify your group submissions
  • Your approximate location such as country or city to tag group origins
  • Interaction data such as likes, clicks, and views stored in Google Sheets
  • Group submissions including link, name, description, image, and category

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or personal identifiers
  • No sign-in or account required
  • No tracking cookies

Third-Party Services

Smart Group Join uses third-party services provided by Google and other providers to operate certain features.

  • Google AdMob Advertising
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Sheets API
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications, if enabled

These services may collect limited technical information as described in their own privacy policies.

Advertising & AdMob

Smart Group Join displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising Identifier GAID or AAID
  • Device information
  • App interaction information
  • Approximate location information, if permitted by device settings

This information may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

Users can control ad personalization through their Google Account and device privacy settings.

Google Ads Policy

How We Use Your Data

  • To display submitted groups to other users after admin approval
  • To enable likes, views, and click counts for ranking
  • To detect your location for local group tagging, if allowed
  • To allow you to manage and delete your own submissions

Referral Program

  • The app may generate a unique referral link linked to a device-generated UserKey
  • Referral statistics, referral counts, and earned coins may be stored in Google Sheets
  • Referral rewards are used only within the app

Coins & Rewards

  • Coins may be awarded through referral milestones
  • Coins can be redeemed to feature approved groups and channels
  • Coin balances are stored in Google Sheets

Community Ranking System

  • Anonymous engagement data such as views, likes, and link opens may be used to calculate group rankings
  • Community activity may contribute to ranking scores
  • No personal identity information is collected for ranking purposes

Push Notifications

If notification permission is granted, Smart Group Join may send notifications related to:

  • Group approvals
  • Group rejections
  • Featured groups
  • Referral rewards
  • Click milestones

Users may disable notifications at any time through device settings or app settings.

Featured Groups

  • Users may spend earned coins to feature their approved groups
  • Featured status may expire automatically after the selected promotion period

Google Sheets Storage

The following app-related information may be stored in Google Sheets:

  • Group submissions
  • Likes, clicks, and views
  • Referral counts
  • Coin balances
  • Feature promotion status
  • Notification records

No personal information such as name, email address, or phone number is required to use the app.

Data Storage

All data is stored in Google Sheets hosted on Google's infrastructure. No custom server database is used. Google's standard data protection applies.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your submitted groups at any time:

  • Delete from the My Groups tab in the app
  • Or contact us at easygroupjoin@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Smart Group Join is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
