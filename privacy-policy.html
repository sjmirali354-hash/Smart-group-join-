Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Smart Group Join respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Randomly generated device key for identifying group submissions
  • Approximate location (country/city level) for content tagging
  • Interaction data such as likes, clicks, and views stored in Google Sheets
  • Group submission data including name, link, description, image URL, and category

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data such as name, email, or phone number
  • No account registration or login system
  • No tracking cookies

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (GAID/IDFA) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can control ad personalization in your device settings. For more information, visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All data is stored in Google Sheets hosted on Google’s infrastructure. We do not use a custom database.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data from the "My Groups" section or contact us at: easygroupjoin@gmail.com

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

For any questions, contact: easygroupjoin@gmail.com
